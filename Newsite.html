Rules for Gold Star Alliance

The Gold Star Alliance is a anti-bullying club that stands for everyone.We want everyone to be happy.

  • We swear to be nice to our peers and everybody around us and show them full respect and avoid mean comments.
  • We will always make sure everyone is treated equally and feels included.
  • We promise to avoid keeping secrets such as mean info, blackmailing about a topic, and game secrets that exclude a person or people. Secrets you may keep include personal info, meeting place and time,and embarrassing stories.
  • We swear to refrain from participating in a fight or cheering or encouraging someone in the fight.
  • If someone makes a gang to pick on someone you tell the mediator (Ms.Overby) and you will kindly ask them to stop.
  • We will always stand for peace.
  • There will be an election for the leader every 2 months.
  • The leader may make new rules but these rules must be voted on by the both classes.
